How to make avocado toast

Assembly time: 5 minutes
Awesome rating: 100% awesome

Are you spending too much at your local coffee shop? Want to know how your snack can still be Instagram worthy at home? Learn how to make your own avocado toast with this simple and delicious recipe!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Bread of your choice - 1 slice
  • Ripe avocado - 1/2
  • Salt and pepper
  • Toaster and utensils
  • Optional: vegetables or egg
  • Optional: seasonings or dressings

  1. Toast your bread until golden and firm

    Usually about 450 degrees Farenheight on a toaster oven for 3-4 minutes or to your preferred liking.

  2. Preparing and spreading your avocado

    Cut your avocado in half, remove the pit, then scoop into a bowl and mash with a fork until smooth. Spread onto toasted bread then season with salt and pepper.

    Basic avocado toast with flaked salt and freshly ground pepper.

  3. Make it your own!

    Add any seasonings, fresh veggies or even an egg.

  4. Enjoy your delicious avocado toast!

    Snap a cute picture and upload to Instagram with a #nofilter. Share with friends and find what you like best. Enjoy!
